Welcome to the Pool! 🌊

Clyst Hydon Swimming Club is a volunteer-run outdoor pool nestled in the beautiful Devon countryside. Open to everyone — come for a refreshing dip, meet friends, or just enjoy the sun on the grass.

The pool is unheated and lovingly maintained by local volunteers. Swim is optional — just come and enjoy the community spirit!

Prices & Membership

Day Ticket: £3.00 per person

Season Ticket: £30.00 per individual

Pre-schoolers & non-swimmers (with a swimmer): Free

We accept cash, card (contactless), cheque or BACS.

Everyone on site must be a member — forms available on request.
